Here are some details on AJCE:

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering (AJCE), located in Kanjirapally, Kerala, India, stands as a distinguished institution renowned for its commitment to academic excellence, holistic development, and innovative education in the field of engineering and technology.
Established in 2001, AJCE has rapidly evolved into a premier technical institution in the region, shaping future engineers and leaders with a strong emphasis on ethical values and societal contributions.

The college emphasizes holistic development and industry-relevant education.
Research and innovation are integral to AJCE's academic ethos.
The college actively promotes research initiatives through collaborations with industries and research organizations.
Students and faculty engage in cutting-edge research across various domains, contributing to advancements in science, technology, and engineering.
It has established partnerships with leading companies, facilitating internships, industrial visits, and placement opportunities for students.
These collaborations ensure that AJCE graduates are not only academically proficient but also industry-ready, with practical skills and knowledge aligned with current market demands.
